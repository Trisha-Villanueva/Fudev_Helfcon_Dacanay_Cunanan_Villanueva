Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my HELF password. How can I reset it?
On the login page, click on "Forgot Password?" and follow the prompts to reset your password using your email address or phone number associated with your account.
How do I create a HELF account?
To create a HELF account, visit the HELF website, fill out the required information in the sign-up form, and follow the on-screen instructions.
